F. How to Make Payments

Bank Payment:
  • Stanbic Bank A/C No: 9030005844648 (Nakasero Branch)
  • Pay at any nearest Stanbic Bank branch or agent.
  • Quote the Student's Name
  • Pay Cash to the Bank Teller or Agent
  • You will get a Payment Receipt confirming your fees payment (School Name, Student Name, School Fees Amount, Charges)
  • Cash payments at school attract a risk fee of shs. 30,000=
  • Money order or bank drafts drawn in the names of the school are acceptable
  • Personal cheques are only acceptable upon the Headteacher’s permission
Mobile Money Payment:
  • MTN Momo: Dial *165*4*3*2*1#
  • Airtel Money: Dial *185*6*2*1#
  • Enter Student Code and Confirm Payment Details.
  • Enter the Student Code. For example: 1003211569
  • Enter the School Fees Amount to Pay to the School.
  • Confirm Details of Payment displayed to you. (School name, Student name, School fees amount, charges).
  • Continue to authorize transaction
  • Enter your Mobile Money PIN.
  • Approve Payment.
  • You will Receive a Message with all your Payment Details.
